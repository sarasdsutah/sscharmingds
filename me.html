The data event that I have been working on...

That is Women in Data Science event in 2021! As a minority group woman in Utah, I started to become an Ambassador for Women in Data Science event since 2019. WiDS Salt Lake City is an independent event organized to coincide with the annual Global Women in Data Science (WiDS) Conference held at Stanford University and an estimated 150+ locations worldwide. All genders are invited to attend WiDS regional events, which features outstanding women doing outstanding work.The Global Women in Data Science (WiDS) Conference aims to inspire and educate data scientists worldwide, regardless of gender, and support women in the field. This one-day technical conference provides an opportunity to hear about the latest data science related research and applications in a broad set of domains.
