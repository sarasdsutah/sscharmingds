The limitation of Data

Mathematics can be challenging for many, but it's an essential discipline to learn, especially in today's world where statistics are frequently used in fields such as politics, economics, and health. However, understanding the real significance behind these numbers can be daunting. For example, the recent news on the potential link between artificial sweeteners and heart disease has gained widespread attention. Nevertheless, it's important to note that the study's findings may be limited as the majority of participants had cardiovascular disease or multiple risk factors for cardiovascular issues, potentially skewing the data. Having worked in data reporting for five years, I strongly believe that understanding the limitations of data is crucial before using it to propose something.
